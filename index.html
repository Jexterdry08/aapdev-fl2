  • Film in Entertainment Industry
    Film is a unique art form that has profoundly impacted how we perceive the world. Filmmakers use techniques like framing, camera angles, and pacing to guide the viewer's emotional and subjective experience, allowing cinema to both immerse the audience and hold them at a distance, enabling critical examination of social norms and ideologies. While cinema can be a dream-like experience, it also has a disruptive potential, challenging the audience through techniques like breaking the fourth wall. Ultimately, learning to critically engage with film allows us to gain new insights and perspectives on the world, as this complex, multifaceted medium both shapes and is shaped by the society and culture it reflects.
